Taigun GT Line

Follow the rainbow? Or follow the birds. Endless exploration starts with the Taigun GT Line thats #StyledToWander. This SUVW is for those who love to discover. Always traversing new roads in, out and way beyond the concrete jungle. Turning every drive into an adventure.

Know more

Taigun GT Plus Sport

Embark on a journey of curious explorations with the Taigun GT Plus Sport. Featuring a dynamic 1.5L TSI EVO engine and your pick of a 6-speed manual or 7-speed DSG transmission, this SUVW invites you to chase excitement wherever the road takes you. With sleek black chrome accents adorning its body, it's the perfect companion for adventurers driving where curiosity leads them to.

Know More

Taigun GT Line

Set forth on an adventure of endless driving in the Taigun GT Line. With its spirited 1.0L TSI EVO engine and the flexibility of a 6-speed manual or automatic transmission, this SUVW carries you to explore new horizons with unmatched curiosity. Stylish black chrome elements bedeck the cars body as you discover the wonders of the open road.

Volkswagen Experiences (VWe)

Open road, wind in your hair and the hum of the tyres on asphalt - for those of you who do not take that just as a joyous driving experience, but as a way of life, VWe exist. The connection is built on an emotion that is far more intrinsic - the love for the drive and the sheer will to always chase the horizon.
